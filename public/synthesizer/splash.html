Using the current tool

Search for possible (partial) pathways to a chemical by entering its standard InChI, a common name, or its Act ID.
For example, for tylenol, you can search for any one of the following:

  1. InChI=1S/C8H9NO2/c1-6(10)9-7-2-4-8(11)5-3-7/h2-5,11H,1H3,(H,9,10)
  2. paracetamol
  3. 12832
